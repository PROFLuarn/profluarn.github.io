Notes for Immigration Officers This document is a confirmed travel itinerary for a group of tourists from Taiwan visiting Germany for sightseeing and cultural activities. All accommodation is pre-booked at the listed hotels. Factory tours (BMW, Audi, Mercedes-Benz) are pre-scheduled guided visits. A brewery tour and restaurant reservation are confirmed for 28 May in Stuttgart (booking ref: qw753I). The group departs Germany on 30 May 2026 via Munich Airport (MUC T2) with return flights to Taoyuan International Airport (TPE), Taiwan.
Important Travel Notes
WeatherHigher rainfall in Bavaria in May, esp. mountain areas. Bring a rain jacket. Sunset ~21:00 in late May. TransportBring Taiwan Driver's License (original) + International Driving Permit. Arrive 15 min early for all tours — late arrivals refused, no refund. Local Tips230V, Type C/F plugs. Check voltage for hair tools. Bring 10,000mAh+ power bank. Shops closed Sundays 5/24 & 5/31 — buy essentials beforehand. VAT refunds at Ingolstadt Village & airport — bring passport copy. Eagle's Nest & Salt MineTunnels 12 degrees C / 54F, humid. Overalls provided — wear warm long sleeves underneath. No sandals — closed grip shoes required. Factory Tour Rules (BMW / Audi / Benz)Footwear: Fully enclosed flat shoes only — no sandals/heels/open-toe or may be denied entry. Long trousers recommended. No photos/videos on production floor — phones may be locked away.
