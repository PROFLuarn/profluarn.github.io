Notes for Immigration Officers This document is a confirmed travel itinerary for a group of tourists from Taiwan visiting Germany for sightseeing and cultural activities. All accommodation is pre-booked at the listed hotels. Factory tours (BMW, Audi, Mercedes-Benz) are pre-scheduled guided visits. A brewery tour and restaurant reservation are confirmed for 28 May in Stuttgart (booking ref: qw753I). The group departs Germany on 30 May 2026 via Munich Airport (MUC T2) with return flights to Taoyuan International Airport (TPE), Taiwan.
Important Travel Notes
Weather — Late May
  • Bavaria has higher rainfall in May (especially in mountain areas). Bring a light rain jacket or waterproof layer.
  • Long daylight hours — sunset around 21:00.
Transport & Driving
  • Bring Taiwan Driver's License (original) + International Driving Permit.
  • All factory tours are pre-booked. Germans are punctual — arrive 15 minutes early. Late arrivals may be refused entry with no refund.
Local Life & Tips
  • 230V power, Type C/F plugs. Hair dryers / curlers must support international voltage.
  • Bring a 10,000mAh+ power bank for long drives and navigation.
  • Sundays (5/24 & 5/31): all supermarkets and shops are closed. Buy essentials beforehand.
  • VAT refunds available at Ingolstadt Village and the airport. Bring a copy of your passport.
Eagle's Nest & Salt Mine
  • Salt mine tunnels are a constant 12°C (54°F) and humid. Protective overalls are provided but wear warm long sleeves or a sweater underneath.
  • Floors are wet and uneven. No sandals or flip-flops — wear closed-toe shoes with good grip.
Factory Tour Rules — BMW · Audi · Mercedes-Benz
Dress Code (Important)
  • Footwear: Fully enclosed flat shoes required (sneakers recommended). No sandals, flip-flops, heels, or open-toe shoes. Non-compliance may result in denied entry.
  • Clothing: Long trousers recommended to protect against bumps or snags on equipment.
Photography Restrictions
  • Absolutely no photos or videos on the production floor.
  • Guides will typically require phones and cameras to be locked in a locker or sealed bag before entering.
