About MediaPipe Hands

MediaPipe Hands can detect up to 2 hands in real-time with 21 3D landmarks per hand. Each finger is color-coded for easy identification: thumb, index, middle, ring, and pinky. The landmarks include wrist, thumb (4 points), index finger (4 points), middle finger (4 points), ring finger (4 points), and pinky (4 points).
